Who Am I...

San Francisco's Lia Rose writes songs that are poetic, intensely honest, and at times unmistakably autobiographical. Her voice has been called “captivating and beautiful” by Tape Op Magazine.

Driven by a fierce optimism and a hope that this world can be a better place for everyone, Rose creates music and tours with projects focused on making a positive impact.

In 2015 Lia Rose relaunched Built for the Sea, signing with Tom Morello's new label: Firebrand Records whose tag line is "We know that music alone does not change the world, but we recognize that it is an integral part of the movements that do." Built for the Sea released a new EP in 2015 on Firebrand Records and they have been playing shows again for the first time since 2009.
